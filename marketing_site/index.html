01

Dispatch Center

Assign calls, balance technician load, prioritize SLA work, and keep office staff aligned on today’s field schedule.

  • Job queue by urgency, client, site, and technician.
  • Status visibility from incoming call to closure.
  • Designed for dispatch desks handling many technicians.
02

AMC Management

Track annual maintenance contracts, SLA terms, renewal dates, contract value, preventive visits, and invoice creation.

  • Active, expired, expiring-soon, on-hold, and cancelled contract states.
  • Monthly and annual contract value with SLA parameters.
  • Renew, create invoice, and review SLA logs from one screen.
03

Quotations

Create service, installation, AMC, and parts quotations that can move into jobs, purchases, and invoices without rework.

  • Client, site, scope, item, tax, and approval readiness.
  • Quote-to-job and quote-to-invoice handoff.
  • Cleaner follow-up for open proposals.
04

GST Invoicing

Raise GST invoices with contract linkage, site details, tax summary, workflow notes, approval actions, and receivable tracking.

  • CGST, SGST, IGST, discount, round-off, and balance due.
  • Invoice activity timeline and quick actions.
  • Operational invoice context before accounting export.
05

Inventory

Control stock used in service jobs, identify low stock, manage reorder levels, and connect preferred vendors to items.

  • Total items, in-stock, low-stock, out-of-stock, and value KPIs.
  • Stock adjustment, transfer, bulk update, and valuation actions.
  • Fast item search, filters, and column controls.
06

CRM

Maintain clients, sites, contacts, service history, GST details, and follow-ups in the same system used by operations.

  • Client and site records tied to contracts and jobs.
  • Service history for faster support conversations.
  • Better renewal and receivable follow-up context.
07

Service Jobs

Convert incidents and customer requests into work orders with equipment, technician, priority, notes, and resolution tracking.

  • Caller, client, site, equipment, priority, and asset capture.
  • Work notes, emails, attachments, and history tabs.
  • Closure and job creation actions for the service desk.
08

Technician Tracking

Know who is assigned, overloaded, delayed, or available before customers start calling for updates.

  • Technician load and open job visibility.
  • Dispatch handoff from office to field execution.
  • Useful for HVAC, electrical, plumbing, and facility teams.
09

WhatsApp Integration

Keep customer communication close to operations for job updates, invoice reminders, AMC renewal messages, and support follow-ups.

  • Send workflow-aware messages from service context.
  • Support renewal and collection reminders.
  • Planned for Indian SME communication habits.
10

Reports & Analytics

Review revenue, overdue receivables, SLA risk, technician load, stock risk, collections, contracts, jobs, and vendors.

  • Command center KPIs for owners and managers.
  • Receivable aging, service load, and revenue trends.
  • Report library across operational modules.
11

Purchase Orders

Manage vendor purchase orders, delivery notes, GST treatment, internal approvals, attachments, and conversion to bills.

  • PO information, vendor details, delivery mode, and project linkage.
  • Approval, print PDF, clone, cancel, and conversion actions.
  • Vendor payables and purchase history context.
12

Vendor Management

Track suppliers, GSTIN, contact details, advances, purchases, payables, and supplier performance for service material control.

  • Vendor master linked to purchase and inventory workflows.
  • Payment and advance tracking.
  • Useful for compressor parts, electrical panels, consumables, and outsourced service work.
